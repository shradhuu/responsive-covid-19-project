B) Coronavirus Transmition

Covid-19 is sperad from person to person mainly through coughing , sneezing and taking and breathing.

1) Droplet

respiratory secretions from coughing or sneezing landing on mucosal surfaces (nose , mouth and eyes)

2) Aerosol

A solid partical or liquid droplet suspended in air

3) Contact

Touching something with SARS-2 virus on it and then touching mouth , nose and eyes

4) Other possible routes

Through fecal matter

5) Incubation period

The incubation period is the time between exposure to a virus and the onset of symptoms.
With COVID-19, symptoms may show 2-14 days after exposure.
CDC indicates that people are most contagious when they are the most symptomatic.
Several studies show people may be contagious before developing symptoms.
